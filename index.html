About Bitlynx Controls

Bitlynx Controls Limited is your trusted partner for IoT Telemetry Systems, Industrial Automation, Process Instrumentation, and Electrical Systems. With over 15 years of technical expertise in IoT Systems, Control Systems, Process Instrumentation, and Electrical Switchgear.

Our passion lies in providing comprehensive turn-key automation solutions that span from factory stand-alone machines to complex industrial and business processes.

Our Motivation

Founded by experienced engineers and managers in the automation space, Bitlynx Controls was established with a clear goal: to fill the critical gap in Control Systems solutions across Tanzania.

We recognized the need for reliable, advanced automation solutions that could meet international standards while being accessible to local industries.

Our Founding Vision

"To bridge the technology gap and bring world-class industrial automation solutions to every corner of Tanzania and beyond."
